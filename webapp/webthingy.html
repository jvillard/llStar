About llStar

llStar is an automatic prover for programs written in bitcode. Bitcode is the intermediate representation of the LLVM compiler framework. llStar attempts to prove individual functions correct with respect to memory safety and to their specifications.

You can find out more about llStar on the llStar wiki, or browse the source code on the bitbucket repository.
